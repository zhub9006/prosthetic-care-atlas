# Prosthetic Care Access Atlas

An open-access resource mapping prosthetic and orthotic care access gaps across underserved regions of the United States, combined with ClinicalTrials.gov prosthetic trial trend analysis.

## Mission

Identify coverage gaps in prosthetic/orthotic care for rural and economically disadvantaged communities, and cross-reference with active clinical research to inform resource allocation and advocacy.

## Regions Covered

| Region | Center Coordinates | Key Challenges |
|--------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Rural West Virginia** | 38.48°N, 80.84°W | Sparse healthcare infrastructure, mountainous terrain, high poverty rates |
| **Eastern Kentucky (Appalachia)** | 37.25°N, 83.20°W | Isolated communities, limited specialist access, opioid epidemic legacy |
| **Mississippi Delta** | 34.00°N, 90.20°W | Extreme poverty, few prosthetics providers, rural infrastructure gaps |
